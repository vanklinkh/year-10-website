Top 5 MTB parks in NZ

  • 1. Queenstown Skyline

    • This Bike park is the best in the country for a good reason, This park has a gondala to take you to the top with no effort needed, the trials at this park are outstandning, the trails are well preserved and the skill difrence varies largley which means this park is good for all riders and their skill levels, also the types of trails vareiy, they have very good jump trails and outstanding tech trails.

  • 2. Rotorua Skyline

    • This bike park is the best in the North Island, they annualy hold Crankworks which is the most popular MTB event in the NZ, This place also has a gondala to the top of the mountain, which is super helpful and convienent, this place also has other attractions on the mountian which is good for if you want a break from biking. they have a resturant, sky swing and a Luge.

  • 3. Port Hills Adventure Park Christchurch

    • This Bike Park is very good for experienced riders, this place has a very large jump trail called OZONE and there are many techinal trails that are challanging. There is also a gondala at this bike park. This park has a mulch jump which is perfect for practicing. You can also hire bikes here.

  • 4. 440 Auckalnd

    • 440 MTb park is a great Place for you and your freinds, they have mutiple vans going up and down the hill which you put your bikes on. This place has a nice restaraunt and very good trails for all levels of biking.

  • 5. Woodhill Auckland

    • Woodhill MTB park is suitable for all skill levels. Although there are no gondalas or shuttles the park is very good and the hills arent too steep. The jump trials there are very good and have mutiple lines for the diffrent skill levels
